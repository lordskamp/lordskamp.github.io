Monitor Color Calibration

Use your iPhone camera as an approximate colorimeter to analyze color accuracy and calculate monitor gamma.

Camera Disclaimer

Phone cameras are not laboratory-grade colorimeters. Factors like auto-exposure, ambient lighting, and screen reflections will impact readings. This tool provides educational, approximate reference values.

How it works

1

Connect Phone

Scan the QR code on the right with your iPhone to establish a secure real-time WebRTC link.

2

Position Sensor

Press the phone camera flat against the center of the screen within the alignment guide.

3

Run Calibration

The screen will flash multiple color patches. Keep the phone steady until completion.
